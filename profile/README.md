## What is Sibelius?

**Sibelius** is the industry-standard music notation software used by composers, arrangers, educators, and musicians worldwide. Developed for creating, editing, and publishing high-quality musical scores, Sibelius combines a powerful set of engraving tools with an intuitive user interface. Whether you are writing a symphony, a film score, a jazz chart, or educational materials, Sibelius provides the precision and flexibility needed for professional results. The software supports a wide range of formats, including MIDI, MusicXML, and PDF, and integrates seamlessly with digital audio workstations (DAWs). With its advanced playback capabilities, realistic sound libraries, and collaborative features, Sibelius is the go-to solution for anyone serious about music notation.
